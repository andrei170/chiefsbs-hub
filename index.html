The Five Pieces

Five installs. Sit on top of the existing chiefsbs.com offer.

No curriculum rebuild. No re-platform. No brand pivot. The five pieces patch the gaps between the 804K audience, the buy decision, the buyer onboarding, and the AEO citation surface.

Piece 01 · Pre-Sell VSL
01

The 5-7 minute educator-register VSL on chiefsbs.com.

The buy decision currently sits on a static page with no operator-voice video calibrated to the regulated-finance educational scope. The 804K-IG audience lands cold on a static surface where the third-party Scam Detector flag has no infrastructure counterweight. We build the 5-7 minute educator-register VSL framed around what the curriculum teaches, who it is for, what the buyer is responsible for, what the independent lender is responsible for, and the explicit outcome-variability statement.

We script the VSL in educational-scope register, direct the record, edit the cut, embed on the chiefsbs.com hero and above the offer-page fold. Voice on screen is Mr. Brown's. Educational scope only.

Compounds against every IG link-in-bio click, every YouTube CTA, every podcast appearance, every press surface that routes traffic to chiefsbs.com.

Piece 02 · Readiness Scorecard
02

The Business Funding Readiness Scorecard between the audience and the buy.

Anyone with a card currently enters the offer at the same friction as a buyer who has built business credit for 3 years. No upstream router, no readiness band classification, no email capture at the qualification step. The fix is a 10-12 question scorecard (time-in-business, revenue band, personal credit band, business credit established, EIN age, documentation readiness, capital need band, current lender relationships, prior coach experience, exit goal) that returns Too Early, Foundation-Building, Mentorship-Ready, or Advanced-Operator plus a 3-line bottleneck readout.

We install the scorecard logic, the routing rules, the bottleneck readouts, the email-capture step, and the band-tagged email integration. Routes fit-mismatched buyers to the right next step before checkout.

Filters fit-mismatched buyers out of the wrong tier (lifts review quality at the source). Captures email at the qualification step. Feeds the AG1-built email sequence the right buyer per band.

Piece 03 · The AEO Citation Infrastructure (The Wedge)
03

The SEO and AEO infrastructure layer that competes for the citation lane.

804K IG followers, real audience-as-authority signal, 0 of 4 high-intent AEO queries cite chiefsbs.com. Competitors with smaller audiences own the citation lane because they have deployed the schema, the FAQ wall, and the legitimacy infrastructure that AI engines and SERP currently reward. The Scam Detector flag has no counterweight on the AEO surface. This is the lead 2026 acquisition-lever leak in the package.

We build Person, Organization, Course, FAQPage, and VideoObject schema across the rebuilt hub and landing; the AEO-optimized FAQ wall calibrated to the 4 high-intent queries; the named-alumni testimonials wall (consent-cleared, educational-outcome language only); the press wall; the regulated-finance footer on every surface. AG1 does not promise rank movement; AG1 builds the infrastructure that competes for the lane.

6 to 12 month compounding asset. Becomes the legitimacy counterweight on the SERP and AEO surface. Compounds against every external content surface that routes traffic to chiefsbs.com.

Piece 04 · Segmented Welcome Flow
04

The buyer-tier segmented welcome flow underneath the existing offer.

Every buyer currently routes into the same downstream regardless of readiness band. No webhook handler on the buy event, no buyer-tier tagging, no forked welcome sequence per band, no documentation-readiness checklist sequence, no expectation reset on what the curriculum teaches versus what independent lenders decide. The fit-mismatched buyer is the buyer who refunds and posts the one-star review that feeds the third-party trust-signal problem.

We wire the Whop / Stripe / GHL webhook handler on the buy event, tag by readiness band, fork the welcome sequence per band, install the documentation-readiness checklist per tier. ConvertKit or GHL + SimpleTexting for the SMS layer.

Lowers refund and chargeback rate at the source. Builds the upsell asset across the 12-month window. Lifts review quality across every band.

Piece 05 · Unified Hub
05

The unified hub on chiefsbs.com that becomes the canonical AEO target.

IG @chiefsbs, IG @sbsmr_.brown, YouTube @CHIEFSBS, and chiefsbs.com currently live on four separate surfaces with no unified hub. AI engines reward operators with a single canonical surface that surfaces every legitimacy signal in one place. The current multi-surface fragmentation splits the trust signal across four surfaces and prevents any one of them from becoming the canonical reference.

We build the unified hub on chiefsbs.com that surfaces the offer, the free training, the named-alumni receipts wall, the schema-marked-up About section, the FAQ wall, and the regulated-finance footer in one canonical surface.

Becomes the canonical AEO citation target. Compounds against every install above and against every external content surface that routes traffic in.
