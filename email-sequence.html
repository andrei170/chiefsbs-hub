Email Sequences - For Mr. Brown (@chiefsbs)

Prepared: 2026-05-17 Scope: Four sequences. Scorecard nurture (forked by readiness band), post-application, buyer-tier segmented welcome, AEO-FAQ opt-in nurture.

All sequences carry the regulated-finance footer. All sequences use educational-scope register. Zero income claims. Zero funding-outcome promises. Zero reproduction of surface bio claims.

Section A - Scorecard Nurture (forked by readiness band)

Trigger: prospect completes the Business Funding Readiness Scorecard. Email captured. Tagged by readiness band: Too Early / Foundation-Building / Mentorship-Ready / Advanced-Operator.

Each band gets a 5-email nurture sequence over 14 days. Below is the Foundation-Building band as the template; the other three bands swap the recommended-next-step content while keeping the same cadence.
