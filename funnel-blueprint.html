The five installs, sequenced

Install 1 - VSL on chiefsbs.com (Bottleneck 01)

Days 1-5. Script, film day, edit, embed on the hub hero and above the offer-page fold. Operator-educator register. Educational-scope only. Locked-claims-only.

Install 2 - Business Funding Readiness Scorecard (Bottleneck 02)

Days 1-5. 10-12 question logic, readiness-band routing, email capture, 3-line bottleneck readout, integration with the scorecard nurture sequence in Section A of email-sequence.md.

Install 3 - SEO + AEO infrastructure layer (Bottleneck 03 - the wedge)

Days 1-7. Person + Organization + Course + FAQPage + VideoObject schema across the AG1-built hub and landing. FAQ wall with the 4 high-intent AEO queries answered in educational-scope register. Named-alumni testimonials wall. Press wall scaffolding (consent-cleared inputs from Mr. Brown's team). Regulated-finance footer on every surface. Lighthouse + Schema Markup Validator pass on day 7.

Install 4 - Segmented welcome flow + scorecard nurture (Bottleneck 04)

Days 3-7. Webhook handler on the buy event, readiness-band tag, fork the welcome sequence per band. Scorecard nurture sequence per band (Section A of email-sequence.md). Buyer-tier segmented welcome (Section C). Optional SMS layer via SimpleTexting on the post-purchase track.

Install 5 - Unified hub + canonical AEO target (Bottleneck 05)

Days 1-7. The hub itself is the canonical AEO target. Schema across the hub, named-alumni wall, press wall, FAQ wall, regulated-finance footer, integration with the existing IG / YouTube / chiefsbs.com surfaces via the routing layer in content-strategy.md.

What the install does NOT include

  • AG1 does not provide funding.
  • AG1 does not script Mr. Brown's audience-facing IG voice.
  • AG1 does not promise rank or AEO citation outcomes; AG1 builds the infrastructure, the rank depends on the broader competitive surface.
  • AG1 does not provide legal review of the disclaimer; Mr. Brown's counsel locks the final language on day 1.
  • AG1 does not run paid ads in the first 7 days; the install is organic infrastructure.

What opens post-install (the 6-12 month compounding window)

  • The AEO infrastructure compounds month-over-month on the SERP and AI-search surfaces.
  • The segmented email list compounds at the rate of scorecard completions.
  • The named-alumni wall compounds at the rate of consent-cleared educational-outcome receipts.
  • The post-purchase segmentation compounds the upsell asset (the Foundation buyer is also the Mentorship and Operator buyer over the 12-month window).

Specific revenue outcomes are not promised. The compounding is on the infrastructure, not on a guaranteed buyer outcome.
